Q3 Floating point

Registers used (registers in pairs) :

### TEMPORARY OR UNUSED REGISTERS ###

$f0-$f2 return registers

$f4 left as N (but saved later anyway)
$f6 used as temporary register
$f8-$f10 unused temporary registers

$f12-$f13 argument registers
$f14 unused argument register

$f16-$f18 unused temporary registers

### SAVED REGISTERS ###

$f20 delta x
$f22 i counter
$f24 N
$f26 x_i
$f28 total sum (cumulative)
